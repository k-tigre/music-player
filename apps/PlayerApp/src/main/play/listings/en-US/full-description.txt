Music Player is a straightforward on-device music player. No accounts or subscriptions — browse your phone library, play in the background, and manage playback the way you expect.

What the app does:
• Catalog: artists → albums → tracks
• Playback queue that persists between sessions
• Playlists and favorite tracks and albums
• Full-screen and mini player with background playback
• Equalizer
• Home screen widgets
• Android Auto: browse library, search, add to queue
• Open audio files from other apps (Downloads, Telegram, and more)

Your music stays on your device — no streaming and no sign-up.
