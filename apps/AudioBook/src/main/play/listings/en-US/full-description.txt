AudioBook is a player for local audiobooks. Point it at your books folder, pick up where you left off, and listen comfortably — including at bedtime.

What the app does:
• Library from a folder on your device (SAF)
• Chapters with saved playback position
• Seek ±15s / ±1min
• Playback speed control
• Sleep timer with smooth volume fade-out
• Extend the timer by shaking or flipping the phone
• Equalizer
• Home screen widgets
• Android Auto: browse books and play
• Background playback via MediaSession

No subscriptions or streaming — just your files on the device.
